About

A different standard in transitional living.

Jade Properties Group was founded on a simple observation: families in transition deserve more than a temporary compromise. When you're building your dream home or relocating for an opportunity, the last thing you need is a residence that feels like a stopgap.

We maintain a deliberately small portfolio of premium homes in Omaha's most desirable neighborhoods. Each property is selected for its quality, thoughtfully prepared, and managed with the same care you'd expect from a five-star concierge — because transitional doesn't have to mean temporary in quality.

Led by Bradly Goodner, our approach is hands-on and detail-oriented. One point of contact. One standard of excellence. Every residence, every resident, every time.
